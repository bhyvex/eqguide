These drop in the Elemental planes, during the Fabled period (typically March 16 - April 16), and are considered among the best, if not the best, weapons for their class for 75-79. Each weapon has a decent ratio, but also procs a swarm pet.

It is also a good idea for any melee class to collect Fabled Ball of Golem Clay from Earth while doing the rounds (drops from Fabled A Korascian Warlord). This gives you an earth elemental illusion, which increases strength and casts root.
